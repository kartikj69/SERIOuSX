Dancing Club

The step up and dance club of srm ktr brings a dance competition to all our fellow college mates where in you will get a chance to showcase those killer moves. The theme of the dance should be based on patriotism since we are celebrating Independence Day.

RULES

The dance video should not be longer than 4 minutes.
Theme is Patriotism.
No entry fees.
Video should be created in Landscape mode with clear lighting.
Video should be uploaded on the google form provided.
