Poetry

The Sahitya club Of SRM is proud to present the most awaited poetry competition. It is providing an opportunity for the young poets of our country to be recognized at a national pedestal. In order to keep the spirits of this independence day high, poems on our beautiful country are welcomed with a warm heart.


Rules

All poems should be uploaded as a word document on the google form provided in the website.
Theme: Patriotism
Word Limit: Maximum 100 words.
Poems should be original. If found to be plagiarized, the contestant will be eliminated immediately.
Plagiarism is frowned upon and must be avoided.
Poems can be in Hindi or English.
